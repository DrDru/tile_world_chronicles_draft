Day 1 : the road to town

Tom rarely goes to town. He used to go there a lot but since he moved in with Didou he does not feel the need to go there anymore.

Despite the sunny weather Tom find it hard to enjoy the three hour walk it takes to go to TOWN.

Something is strange. After walking two hours Tom has not seen anyone. A traveling merchant shows up. He may be able to help.
