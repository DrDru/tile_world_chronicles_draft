Tom's adventure

The Tile world has been a peaceful place for as long as people remember but the future is uncertain.

In various places people have started disappearing. Everywhere fear is growing.

Tom does not know about it. His fiance has been missing for two weeks. He has searched everywhere and waited but has seen no sign of her.

He has finally decided to leave is house by the forest and go to town to ask. Maybe someone would have seen her.
